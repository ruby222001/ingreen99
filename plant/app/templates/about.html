About Us


At Ingreen99, we are passionate about bringing the beauty of nature into your home. We believe that plants have the power to transform any space, creating a serene and vibrant atmosphere. Whether you're an experienced gardener or just starting your plant journey, we're here to help you find the perfect plants to enhance your living environment. With years of experience in the plant industry, we have curated a diverse selection of premium-quality plants that are not only visually stunning but also thrive in a variety of indoor and outdoor settings. Our team of dedicated horticultural experts carefully handpick each plant, ensuring that they are healthy, well-rooted, and ready to thrive in their new homes. We understand that each plant lover has unique preferences and needs, which is why we offer a wide range of plant species, sizes, and styles. From lush tropical foliage to elegant succulents, fragrant flowers to air-purifying greenery, we have something to suit every taste and space.
